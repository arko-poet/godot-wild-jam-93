# Nebula Bounty Hunters

Manage stagecoaches and bounty hunters to collect stars before your competition does.

## About
**Nebula Bounty Hunters** was created for the [**Godot Wild Jam #93**](https://itch.io/jam/godot-wild-jam-93) in 9 days by a team of 4 developers.

In this real time strategy-management game you:
- Assign bounty hunters to stagecoaches
- Dispatch crews to collect stars
- Claim bounties in town
- Maximize profits before time runs out

## Controls
- **LMB** — Interact with stagecoaches, stars, town, and UI
- **RMB** — Cancel selection
- **ESC** — Open options menu
